I am a tenure-track assistant professor at the Lally School of Management, Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute Lally School of Management, Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute (RPI). My broad research areas include Social Computing, Machine Learning and Crowdsourced Planning. I received my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from Arizona State University under the supervision of Prof. Subbarao Kambhampati. Before ASU, I pursued M.S by thesis and B.Tech (Honours) in Computer Science from IIIT-Hyderabad under the supervision of Prof. Vikram Pudi.
